How do I know what kind of skin I have?

Here are some features to identify your skin type, we add pages that facilitate your search, however remember that you must go with a dermatologist before experimenting on your skin.

  • Oily Skin: Shiny, oily and with visible pores
  • Mixed Skin: Oily and dry areas, pores visible in some areas
  • Dry Skin: Tight, rough and with little visible pores
  • Sensitive skin: With redness and irritation
